The Tracer Eco-System

The Tracer protocol is a DAO governed network of financial smart contracts. The Factory smart contract can host a any standardised derivative, lending and borrowing, exchange and other financial contracts. These contracts can then be permissionlessly deployed from the factory and utilised by any market participants with an internet connection.
